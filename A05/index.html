About Boracay

Located in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines, Boracay is a small island renowned for its powdery white sands, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant nightlife. It's consistently ranked among the best beach destinations in the world.

Whether you're looking to relax or seek adventure, Boracay has something for every traveler.

Top Attractions

White Beach

The heart of Boracay – known for its 4km stretch of soft white sand and calm waters.

Puka Shell Beach

Less crowded and more peaceful, famous for its natural puka shells and turquoise waves.

Willy's Rock

An iconic volcanic rock formation with a statue of the Virgin Mary – a popular photo spot.
